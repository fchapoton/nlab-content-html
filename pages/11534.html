Idea

By the Burnside category of a finite group GG one means either

  1. the category of correspondences in finite G-sets

  2. or the abelianization of that

    1. either by group completion of its monoidal hom-groupoids to an additive 1-category;

    2. or by forming the K-theory of a permutative category of the hom-groupoids to a spectra-enriched (∞,1)-category.

Then functors/additive functors/enriched (∞,1)-functors from the Burnside category to, in particular, the category of abelian groups/(∞,1)-category of spectra are called Mackey functors.

Definition

Proposition

(the permutative category of finite G-sets)

For GG be a finite group, write GFinSet skG FinSet_{sk} for the skeleton of the category of finite G-sets.

Its objects may be identified with pairs (n,ρ)(n,\rho) consisting of a natural number nn, reflecting the finite set {1,2,⋯,n}\{1,2, \cdots, n\}, and a group homomorphism ρ:G⟶S n=Aut({1,2,⋯,n})\rho \;\colon\; G \longrightarrow S_n = Aut(\{1,2, \cdots, n\}) from GG to the symmetric group on nn elements, reflecting the automorphism group of that finite set.

Its morphisms (n 1,ρ 1)⟶f(n 2,ρ 2)(n_1,\rho_1) \overset{ f }{\longrightarrow} (n_2, \rho_2) are functions f:{1,2,⋯,n 1}⟶{1,2,⋯,n 2}f \;\colon\; \{1, 2, \cdots, n_1\} \longrightarrow \{1,2, \cdots, n_2\} that intertwine ρ 1\rho_1 and ρ 2\rho_2.

The coproduct ⊔\sqcup of G-sets (disjoint union) makes this skeleton a permutative category (GFinSet sk,⊔)\big( G FinSet_{sk}, \sqcup \big).

In the same way its slice category over any object X∈GFinSet skX \in G FinSet_{sk} becomes a permutative category (GFinSet sk/ X,⊔)\big( G FinSet_{sk}/_{X}, \sqcup \big) under disjoint union of domains.

Similarly, the Cartesian product of finite GG-sets can be restricted to these skeleta to produce bipermutative categories (GFinSet sk/ X,⊔,×)\big( G FinSet_{sk}/_{X}, \sqcup, \times \big).

Finally, restriction to isomorphisms (passage to cores) yields the bipermutative groupoids (Core(GFinSet sk)/ X,⊔,×)\big( Core(G FinSet_{sk})/_{X}, \sqcup, \times \big).

(e.g. Guillou-May 11, Def. 1.3, Bohmann-Osorno 14, Def. 1.4)

Definition

(locall permutative Burnside category)

The (2,1)-category of correspondences Corr(GFinSet)Corr(G FinSet) is equivalent to the (2,1)-category whose objects are the finite G-sets and whose hom-categories are the permutative cores of skeleta of slice categories of G-sets from Def. , over the Cartesian product of source and domain G-sets:

Corr(GFinSet)(S 1,S 2)≃Core(GFinSet sk/ S 1×S 2). Corr(G FinSet)\big( S_1, S_2 \big) \;\simeq\; Core(G FinSet_{sk}/_{S_1 \times S_2}) \,.

This locally skeletal (2,1)-category is the locally permutative version GℰG \mathcal{E} of the Burnside category.

It may be regarded as an enriched category over the multicategory PermPerm of permutative categories (a PC\mathbf{PC}-catgeory in the sense of Guillout 10)

(Guillou-May 11, Def. 1.6, Bohmann-Osorno 14, Def. 7.1, 7.2)
