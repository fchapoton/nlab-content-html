A kind of spacetime. For the moment see at KK-monopole for more.

Consider the left invariant 1-forms on the 3-sphere ≃\simeq SU(2), which in terms of Euler angles? are

σ 1 =sinψdθ−cosψsinθdϕ σ 2 =cosψdθ+sinψsinθdϕ σ 3 =dψ+cosθdϕ \begin{aligned} \sigma_1 &= \sin \psi \, d \theta - \cos \psi \sin \theta \, d \phi \\ \sigma_2 & = \cos \psi \, d \theta + \sin \psi \sin \theta \, d \phi \\ \sigma_3 &= d \psi + \cos \theta \, d \phi \end{aligned}
