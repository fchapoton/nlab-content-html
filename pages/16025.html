Idea

A conformal connection is a (H↪G)(H \hookrightarrow G)-Cartan connection for G=O +(n,1)G= O^+(n,1) an orthochronous Lorentz group and HH the stabilizer subgroup of a lightlike line in Minkowski space ℝ n,1\mathbb{R}^{n,1}. Here G/HG/H is the celestial sphere of dimension n−1n-1.
