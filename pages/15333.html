Idea

Given a ringed space/ringed topos (X,𝒪 X)(X, \mathcal{O}_X), with (commutative) structure sheaf 𝒪 X\mathcal{O}_X, then the sheaf of rational functions K XK_X associated with this data is the sheafification of the presheaf obtained from the structure sheaf by localizing its values 𝒪 X(U)\mathcal{O}_X(U) at the multiplicative set of all f∈𝒪 X(U)f \in \mathcal{O}_X(U) which are stalkwise not zero-divisors.

On an integral scheme this amounts to forming fields of fractions of the regular function?s.
