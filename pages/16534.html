Brane webs

Intersections of branes of equal dimension may form regular patterns known as brane webs. This applies notably in type IIB string theory where (p,q)-strings may form “string webs” (references below) and (p,q)5-branes may form “5-brane webs” (references further below)

General

  • Oleg Lunin, Brane webs and 1/4-BPS geometries, JHEP09 (2008) (arXiv:0802.0735)

String webs

  • Ashoke Sen, String Network, JHEP 9803:005, 1998 (arXiv:hep-th/9711130)

  • Soo-Jong Rey, Jung-Tay Yee, BPS Dynamics of Triple (p,q)(p,q) String Junction, Nucl.Phys. B526 (1998) 229-240 (arXiv:hep-th/9711202)

  • Keshav Dasgupta, Sunil Mukhi, BPS Nature of 3-String Junctions, Phys.Lett. B423 (1998) 261-264 (arXiv:hep-th/9711094)

Via tropical geometry:
