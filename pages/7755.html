Idea

A class of structures in model theory has the amalgamation property if for any three structures A,B,CA,B,C and embeddings f B:A↪Bf_B: A\hookrightarrow B, f C:A↪Cf_C: A\hookrightarrow C, there exist embeddings g B:B↪Dg_B:B\hookrightarrow D and g C:C↪Dg_C: C\hookrightarrow D such that g B∘f B=g C∘f Cg_B\circ f_B = g_C\circ f_C.

One of the simplest cases is when the free amalgam of structures B⊕ ACB\oplus_A C exists.
