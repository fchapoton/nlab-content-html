Idea

For any 2-category, KK, there is a 2-category Adj(K)Adj(K) composed of the objects of KK, adjunctions in KK, and adjunction morphisms.

Adj(Adj(K))Adj(Adj(K)) is formed by the adjoint triples of KK.

Morphisms in Adj(K)Adj(K) are 2-functors from Adj to KK. The inclusion of MndMnd, the free monad, in AdjAdj induces a 2-functor from the 2-category of adjunctions in KK to the 2-category of monads in KK. The adjoints to this 2-functor are the Kleisli and Eilenberg-Moore constructions on monads in KK.
