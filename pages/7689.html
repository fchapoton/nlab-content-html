Definition

A not necesarily commutative connected formal group G=SpfAG=\Sp f A is called smooth formal k-group? if AA is a power-series algebra k[[X 1,...X n]]k[ [X_1,...X_n] ] in nn variables

The coproduct Δ:A→A⊗^A\Delta:A\to A\hat \otimes A is given by a set af formal power series Φ(X,Y)=(Φ i(X 1,...,X n,Y 1,...,Y n)),i 1,...,n\Phi(X,Y)=(\Phi_i(X_1,..., X_n, Y_1,..., Y_n)), i_1,...,n satisfying the axioms (Ass),(Un) and (Com). Such a set {Φ i}\{\Phi_i\} is called a Dieudonné group law.
