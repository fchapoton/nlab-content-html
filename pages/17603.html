hyperalgebra

Hyperalgebra of an affine algebraic group GG is the finite duals of the Hopf algebra of representative functions of GG. It can be interpreted (and is sometimes called) the algebra of distributions supported at unit. This algebra comes with a natural filtration. In characteristic 00 it coincides (by L. Schwarz’s theorem) with the universal enveloping algebra of the Lie algebra of GG, but it is much bigger in positive characteristic. It can also be obtained by base change from the Kostant’s integral form of the universal enveloping algebra of the complex Lie algebra associated to GG.

Some books on algebraic groups and on Hopf algebras have chapters dedicated to this topic e.g.

  • M. Sweedler, Hopf algebras, Benjamin, NY 1969
  • C. Jantzen, Representations of algebraic groups, chapter 7
  • Η. Yanagihara, Theory of Hopf algebras attached to group schemes, Springer Lecture Notes in Mathematics 614 (1977)
