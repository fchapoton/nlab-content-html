  • David Ben-Zvi,

    Loop Groups, Characters and Elliptic Curves

    talk at ASPECTS of Topology, 17-19 December 2012, Oxford,

    (pdf slides)

on (geometric) representation theory reflected in and incarnated as low-dimensional extended gauge field theory, some relevant categorification in D-geometry (e. g. the Hecke category) and specifically on formalizations of geometric Langlands duality in terms of 2-dimensional QFT motivated from compactification of N=4 D=4 super Yang-Mills theory.

(Other talks at the same conference include Dan Freed: 4-3-2 8-7-6.)
