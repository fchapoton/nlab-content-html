Here we collect all the rules, definitions, and inductive clauses that pertains specifically to Π\Pi-types.

Raw syntax

  • If A,BA,B are raw terms and xx is a variable, then Π(x:A).B\Pi(x:A).B is a raw term.
  • If A,B,MA,B,M are raw terms and xx is a variable, then λ(x:A.B).M\lambda(x:A.B). M is a raw term.
  • If A,B,M,NA,B,M,N are raw terms and xx is a variable, then App x:A.B(M,N)App^{x:A.B}(M,N) is a raw term.
