Example

(field bundle for B-field)

On Minkowski spacetime Σ\Sigma (def. \ref{MinkowskiSpacetime}), let the field bundle (def. \ref{FieldsAndFieldBundles}) be given by the skew-symmetrized tensor product of vector bundles of the cotangent bundle with itself

E≔∧ Σ 2T *Σ. E \coloneqq \wedge^2_\Sigma T^\ast \Sigma \,.

This is a trivial vector bundle (example \ref{TrivialVectorBundleAsAFieldBundle}) with canonical field coordinates (b μν)(b_{\mu \nu}) subject to

b μν=−b νμ. b_{\mu \nu} \;=\; - b_{\nu \mu} \,.

A section of this bundle, hence a field history, is a differential 2-form (def. \ref{DifferentialnForms})

B∈Γ Σ(∧ Σ 2T *Σ)=Ω 2(Σ) B \in \Gamma_\Sigma(\wedge^2_\Sigma T^\ast \Sigma) = \Omega^2(\Sigma)

on spacetime.
