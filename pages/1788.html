Definition

(vertical cotangent bundle)

Let E→fbΣE \overset{fb}{\to} \Sigma be a fiber bundle. Its vertical cotangent bundle

T Σ *E ⟶ E ↘ ↙ Σ \array{ T^\ast_\Sigma E && \longrightarrow && E \\ & \searrow && \swarrow \\ && \Sigma }

is the fiber bundle whose fiber over x∈Σx \in \Sigma is the cotangent bundle of the fiber of EE over XX:

(T Σ *E) x≔T *E x. \left( T^\ast_\Sigma E \right)_x \;\coloneqq\; T^\ast E_x \,.

In the case that E→fbΣE \overset{fb}{\to} \Sigma is a vector bundle, this is the fiber product of EE with its dual vector bundle

T Σ *E≃E×ΣE *. T^\ast_\Sigma E \;\simeq\; E \underset{\Sigma}{\times} E^\ast \,.

In the case that E=Σ×FE = \Sigma \times F is a trivial bundle, then

T Σ *E≃Σ×T *F. T^\ast_\Sigma E \simeq \Sigma \times T^\ast F \,.

Finally if E=Σ×VE = \Sigma \times V is both trivial and a vector bundle, then

T Σ *E≃Σ×V×V *, T^\ast_\Sigma E \;\simeq\; \Sigma \times V \times V^\ast \,,

where V *V^\ast is the dual vector space of VV.
