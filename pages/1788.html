Moreover, by prop. \ref{DiracSpaceOfFieldHistories} we have that the coordinate functions on the space of field histories of the Dirac bundle are given by the field observables Ψ α(x)\mathbf{\Psi}^\alpha(x) regarded in odd degree. Now a homomorphism as above has to pull back the even coordinate function xx on ℂ\mathbb{C} to even coordinate functions on this Cartesian product, hence to joint even powers of θ\theta and Ψ α(x)\mathbf{\Psi}^\alpha(x).
