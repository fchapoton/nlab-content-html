Idea

A conjecture in chromatic homotopy theory about the nature of the algebraic K-theory spectrum K(R)K(R) of an E-infinity ring RR. Roughly, the conjecture says that K(R)K(R) has chromatic level one higher than RR.

References

The conjecture as such is stated in

  • Christian Ausoni, John Rognes, The chromatic red-shift in algebraic K-theory, Enseign. Math. (2) 54 (2008), 9-11. (pdf)

an outlook as of 2013 is in section 3 of

  • Tyler Lawson, The future, Talbot lectures 2013 (pdf)

Previous work motivating it was the study of the algebraic K-theory K(KU)K(KU) of the complex K-theory spectrum KUKU (also thought of as the classifying space for BDR 2-vector bundles) in

  • Nils Baas, Bjørn Dundas, Birgit Richter, John Rognes, Ring completion of rig categories (arXiv:0706.0531)

which was motivated by the desire to turn topological K-theory into “a form of” elliptic cohomology by a kind of categorification.

See also

  • Christian Ausoni, On the algebraic K-theory of the complex K-theory spectrum (arXiv:0902.2334)
