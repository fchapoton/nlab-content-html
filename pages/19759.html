Idea

The finite general linear group GL(2,𝟛)GL(2,\mathbb{3}) for coefficients the prime field 𝔽 3\mathbb{F}_3.

Properties

Character table

The character table for GL(2,𝔽 3)GL(2,\mathbb{F}_3) over the complex numbers looks like that of the binary octahedral group. The only difference is the Schur indices. Hence the character tables over the real numbers do differ.
