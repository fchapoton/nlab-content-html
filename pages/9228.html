Definition

For μ\mu a probability measure on the real line ℝ\mathbb{R}, its nnth moment for n∈ℕn \in \mathbb{N} is the expectation value of x nx^n.

Properties

  • Moments of Gaussian probability distributions have a combinatorial expression by Wick's lemma.
