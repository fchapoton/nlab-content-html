Idea

A potential quantum anomaly in M-theory/type IIA string theory.

References

General

The anomaly was found in

  • Diaconescu, Gregory Moore, Edward Witten, E 8E_8 Gauge Theory, and a Derivation of K-Theory from M-Theory, Adv.Theor.Math.Phys.6:1031-1134,2003 (arXiv:hep-th/0005090)

while mostly assuming vanishing NS-fields (the B-field). The non-vanihshing B-field in this context was considered in

  • Gregory Moore, Natalia Saulina, T-Duality, and the K-Theoretic Partition Function of TypeIIA Superstring Theory, Nucl.Phys. B670 (2003) 27-89 (arXiv:hep-th/0206092)

where however the anomaly as such is not discussed. This was then put together in

  • Varghese Mathai, Hisham Sati, Some Relations between Twisted K-theory and E8 Gauge Theory, JHEP0403:016,2004 (arXiv:hep-th/0312033)

which hence discusses the anomaly for twisted K-theory.

Interpretaton as Orientation in second integral Morava K-theory

An interpretation of the phenomenon in terms of orientation in second integral Morava K-theory is in

  • Igor Kriz, Hisham Sati, M-theory, type IIA superstrings, and elliptic cohomology, Adv. Theor. Math. Phys. 8 (2004), no. 2, 345–394 (Euclid, arXiv:hep-th/0404013)
  • Lukas Buhné, Properties of Integral Morava K-Theory and the Asserted Application to the Diaconescu-Moore-Witten Anomaly, Diploma thesis Hamburg (2011)
