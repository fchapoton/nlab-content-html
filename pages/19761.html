Idea

For ζ n\zeta_n an nnth root of unity and kk a field, consider the cyclotomic field k(ζ n)k(\zeta_n). The ring of integers of this field is called the ring of cyclotomic integers.

Examples

  • For ℚ\mathbb{Q} the rational numbers, consider the cyclotomic field ℚ(ζ n)\mathbb{Q}(\zeta_n). The ring of integers of this field is ℤ[ζ n]\mathbb{Z}[\zeta_n], i.e. polynomials in ζ n\zeta_n with integer coefficients.

  • characters are cyclotomic integers
