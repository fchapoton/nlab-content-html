Idea

A supergeometric analog of anti-de Sitter spacetime. By the discussion at supersymmetry – Classification – Superconformal and super anti de Sitter symmetry this includes the following coset superspacetimes:

ddsuper anti de Sitter spacetime
4OSp(8/4)Spin(3,1)×SO(7)\;\;\;\;\frac{OSp(8/4)}{Spin(3,1) \times SO(7)}\;\;\;\;
5SU(2,2/5)Spin(4,1)×SO(5)\;\;\;\;\frac{SU(2,2/5)}{Spin(4,1)\times SO(5)}\;\;\;\;
7OSp(6,2/4)Spin(6,1)×SO(4)\;\;\;\;\frac{OSp(6,2/4)}{Spin(6,1) \times SO(4)}\;\;\;\;

Notice that the de Sitter spacetime (not anti-de Sitter) does not have a standard extension to supergeometry, but see arXiv:1610.01566.
